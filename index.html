The AP Exam at a glance

The exam is about 3 hours 15 minutes and has two equally weighted sections:

SectionQuestionsCalculator?Weight
I – Multiple Choice45 questionsPart A: no · Part B: yes50%
II – Free Response6 questionsPart A: yes · Part B: no50%

The exam is held in early-to-mid May — College Board sets the exact date each year.

The 8 units

The percentage on each card is how much of the exam that unit covers — spend your time accordingly.
